

                            KC-135T KEY COMMANDS

 Keys                   Function      

 Shift + 1             Don't Use
 Shift + 2 + 3      Toggles Copilots Panel 
 Shift + 3 + 2      Toggles Pilots Panel   
 Shift + 4          Toggles Engine Start Panel 
 Shift + 5          Toggles A/P 
 Shift + 6          Toggles Fuel Panel
 Shift + 7          Toggles Radiostack
 Shift + 8          Toggles GPS
 shift + 9          Toggles Throttle Quadrant 

              VIEWS

 shift + KP1        Rear Left
 Shift + KP2        Rear
 Shift + KP3        Rear Right
 Shift + KP4        Left
 Shift + KP5        Boom Operaters Sighting Window
 Shift + KP6        Right
 Shift + KP7        Forward Left
 Shift + KP8        Pilots Panel
 Shift + KP9        Forward Right
 
 Ctrl + KP1         Left Wing
 Ctrl + KP3         Right Wing
 Ctrl + KP8         Overhead Panel           
 Ctrl + KP5         Landing Panel
   
                     TO OPEN AIRCRAFT DOORS
 
To open doors use "SHIFT+E" for Passenger door and "SHIFT+E" then "2" for Cargo door.
