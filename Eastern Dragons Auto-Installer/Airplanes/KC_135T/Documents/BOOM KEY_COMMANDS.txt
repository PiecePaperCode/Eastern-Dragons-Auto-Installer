                   


                TO SET UP KEY COMMANDS FOR EXTENDING/RETRACTING
                THE REFUELING BOOM.
 
 1. Open FS9 or FS2K2,press the ALT KEY then options,CONTROLS,ASSIGNMENTS.
 
 2. Under the BUTTONS/KEY TAB in the "Assignment List" Screen scholl down
    and find the "TAILHOOK" listing. Assign the "SHIFT + T" Keys, click OK.


    Now you will be able to EXTEND and RETRACT the Boom by pressing the 
    "Shift + T" key.
